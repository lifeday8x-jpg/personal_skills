# Style Profile

## Visual Thesis

"Eastern fantasy modern ritual UI": an immortal-cultivation diagnostic instrument presented with the clarity of a mobile game interface.

The player should feel they are using a sacred apparatus, not filling in a personality test.

## Primary Motifs

- Lingjian mirror, root-bone scanner, fate astrolabe, circular jade gate.
- Jade discs, aged brass rings, blackened metal, pale stone, glassy teal light.
- Fine gold measurement lines, compass ticks, star chart arcs, talisman glyphs.
- Ink mountains, cloud sea, moon, brush swirls, remote pavilions, ritual platform.
- Translucent body silhouette with bones, meridians, light points, scanning rings.

## Palette

- Base: mist white, ink gray, pale stone, deep green-black.
- Accent: celadon jade, muted teal glow, aged brass/gold.
- Effect colors: controlled elemental variation only when needed.
- Avoid dominant red/gold, saturated purple, neon cyberpunk blue, heavy brown/orange.

## Materials

- Jade should feel translucent and mineral, not plastic.
- Brass/gold should be thin ornamental linework, not casino luxury.
- Stone should be pale, worn, ritual-clean.
- Ink should be atmospheric and layered, not dirty or muddy.

## Composition

- Prefer centered circular apparatus, clear ritual symmetry, and vertical 9:16 framing.
- Keep lower 20-30% available for buttons/cards/results.
- Keep central device readable at phone size.
- Use background mountains as depth, not as detail competition.
- Use thin UI glyphs around large readable forms.

## Avoid

- Ordinary quiz/personality-test card layout.
- Page-game red and gold excess.
- Tarot-card cliché, generic horoscope app, medical sci-fi scanner.
- Overly character-led poster composition.
- Tiny unreadable text baked into images.
- Overfilled ornaments that cannot become tappable UI elements.
