# Asset Types

## vertical-main-screen

Purpose: first playable screen or key concept.

Must include:

- Center Lingjian or astrolabe apparatus.
- Clear ritual stage or platform.
- Bottom interaction zone with 1-3 button/card placeholders.
- Background depth from Shanhai ink mountains.
- No baked questionnaire copy.

Good prompt additions:

- `9:16 mobile game UI concept`
- `large readable touch controls`
- `lower third reserved for jade interaction buttons`
- `layered interface, no text`

## lingjian-device

Purpose: reusable central machine or scanning prop.

Variants:

- Human scan mirror.
- Empty circular gate.
- Floating astrolabe ring.
- Tabletop bone-reading platform.

Production constraints:

- Ask for isolated object or transparent background when possible.
- Generate front-facing and slightly top-down variants separately.
- Keep silhouette simple enough to mask.

## root-bone-result-card

Purpose: result presentation after scan.

Components:

- Result frame/background.
- Grade emblem.
- Name/title plaque without baked text.
- Elemental glow variant.
- Optional translucent body silhouette.

Avoid making it look like a horoscope/tarot card unless explicitly requested.

## ritual-button

Purpose: tappable UI controls.

States:

- Idle jade.
- Active glowing jade.
- Disabled pale stone.
- Pressed darker rim.

Keep icons simple: flower knot, star point, spiral, seal mark, elemental glyph.

## astrolabe-ui

Purpose: overlay rings, selection dials, loading/scanning animation source.

Generate as:

- Ring segments.
- Compass ticks.
- Star node network.
- Talisman glyph circle.
- Rotating halo layer.

Use transparent background and centered square canvas when possible.

## energy-effect

Purpose: VFX source material.

Types:

- thunder: thin gold-white cracks, disciplined, not explosive.
- wood: green meridian vines, soft growth lines.
- water: ink ripple, moonlit waves, teal shimmer.
- fire: pale ritual flame, not orange inferno.
- star: star dust, constellation glints.
- shadow: ink veil, smoky brush stroke.

## ink-shanhai-background

Purpose: reusable background plate.

Keep separate from foreground UI. Avoid strong focal objects in the lower UI zone.

## spirit-bone-silhouette

Purpose: scanning human figure.

Requirements:

- Androgynous, translucent, no identity-specific face.
- Visible bones/meridians/light nodes.
- Upright front pose, arms slightly open.
- Works over dark teal or pale mist background.
