# Style Profile

## Visual Thesis

"Eastern fantasy modern ritual UI": a sacred instrument interface presented with the clarity of a mobile game.

The player should feel they are using a mysterious apparatus, not filling in a personality test.

This profile defines the **style envelope** — applicable to any game concept that fits within this aesthetic.

## Primary Motifs (Style Elements)

- Circular jade gates, aged brass rings, ritual measurement instruments.
- Jade discs, blackened metal, pale stone, glassy teal light.
- Fine gold measurement lines, compass ticks, star chart arcs, talisman glyphs.
- Ink mountains, cloud sea, moon, brush swirls, remote pavilions, ritual platforms.
- Translucent scanning/reading overlays with internal structure and light points.

## Palette

- **Base**: mist white, ink gray, pale stone, deep green-black.
- **Accent**: celadon jade, muted teal glow, aged brass/gold.
- **Effect colors**: controlled elemental variation only when needed.
- **Avoid**: dominant red/gold, saturated purple, neon cyberpunk blue, heavy brown/orange.

## Materials

- **Jade**: translucent and mineral, not plastic.
- **Brass/gold**: thin ornamental linework, not casino luxury.
- **Stone**: pale, worn, ritual-clean.
- **Ink**: atmospheric and layered, not dirty or muddy.

## Composition Rules

- Prefer centered circular apparatus, clear ritual symmetry, vertical 9:16 framing.
- Keep lower 20-30% available for buttons/cards/results.
- Keep central device readable at phone size.
- Use background mountains as depth, not as detail competition.
- Use thin UI glyphs around large readable forms.

## Avoid (Hard Rules)

- Ordinary quiz/personality-test card layout.
- Page-game red and gold excess.
- Tarot-card cliché, generic horoscope app, medical sci-fi scanner.
- Overly character-led poster composition.
- Tiny unreadable text baked into images.
- Overfilled ornaments that cannot become tappable UI elements.
- Neon cyberpunk aesthetics.
- Explosive/violent VFX (energy should be disciplined and calm).
